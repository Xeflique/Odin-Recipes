Description

Lasagna is a tipically italian recipe, made with beef and tomato sauce on top of lasagna pasta sheets and with bechamel sauce. A fun a delicious meal for all the family.

Ingredients:

  • 2 sp. virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 500g of minced beef
  • 1 tea sp. of salt
  • 3 tomatos
  • 100ml of tomato polp
  • 12 sheets of lasagna pasta
  • 80g of mozzarella cheese
  • 700ml of bechamel sauce

Steps:

  1. Pre hea the oven at 180ºC.

  2. In a pot, lightly fry the onion and the gloves of garlic in olive oil. Add the minced beef and let it cook for at least 3 minutes in medium heat always stirring.

  3. Next, add cooking wine and add the oreganos. Season with half black pepper half salt.

  4. Add the diced tomatos, cleaned of all the seeds and peeled, along with the tomato polp.

  5. Let it cook for about 10 minutes in medium heat stirring from time to time till it dries a little. Take it from the heat and keep it.

  6. For the bechamel sauce: add the bechamel sauce in a pot in low heat and be always stirring so it doesn't stick.

  7. In a square oven tray, put in layers: the sheets of the sligtly cooked lasagna pasta, beef and tomato and the bechamel sauce. Repeat the process, ending with lasagna pasta sheets.

  8. Sprinkle with mozzarella chesse and put it in the oven at 190ºC for 25 minutes.
